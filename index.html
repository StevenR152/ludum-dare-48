Ludum Dare #48

Well, you had a good run as a Pharaoh, Esophagus. You were benevolent, your people loved you, and you lived a rich and powerful life. But all things must come to an end one day… Unless you can access eternal peace?

For that, you will have to go deeper and deeper and… well, deeper. Your sarcophagus is waiting for you… But quick! Explorers are on their way and Osiris is waiting for your offerings!

Instructions

  • Use arrow keys to move your character around.
  • Space bar will pull any levers nearby.
  • Keep an eye out for offerings for Osiris on the way.
